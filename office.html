NEW PROJECT: Otherwhere
Take your adventure to the dungeon that is neverending or is it? You don't have to fear the dungeon has you covered with a DUNGEON FRAME, a robot of your own to take the damage, hopefully you can afford it if it breaks.

For this game I wanted a neverending approach with a few story elements.
To have a player fail, and have a permament fail or a chance at redemption.
To have the player earn currencies through quests, gather materials, finding artifacts, treasures, and other mysteries objects.
Find the correct path and discover the truth behind the neverending Otherwhere dungeon!
